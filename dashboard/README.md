## Key Metrics Explained

**HHI (Herfindahl-Hirschman Index):**
- < 0.15: Competitive
- 0.15-0.25: Moderate concentration
- 0.25-0.50: High concentration
- > 0.50: Monopolistic

**Price War Detection:**
- Price std dev > $5 indicates active price competition

**Market Structure:**
- Auto-classified based on HHI and share distribution

**Strategy Classification:**
- Innovation Leader: High R&D investment
- Price Warrior: Low margins, aggressive pricing
- Generic Follower: Low innovation, moderate prices
